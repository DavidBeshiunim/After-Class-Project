About Hiking

Hiking is a recreational activity enjoyed by millions of people around the world. It involves walking on trails or paths in natural environments, often in mountains, forests, or parks.

Popular Trails

  • Mount Kilimanjaro, Tanzania
  • Appalachian Trail, United States
  • Mount Everest Base Camp, Nepal

Hiking Tips

  • Wear appropriate clothing and footwear.
  • Stay hydrated and bring enough water.
  • Carry a map and compass, and know how to use them.
  • Leave no trace - pack out what you pack in.
